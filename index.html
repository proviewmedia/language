Greetings & Basics

Ordering Food

Menu vocabulary, asking for the check, dietary needs. Eat like a local, not a tourist pointing at pictures.

Asking Directions

Where is...? How do I get to...? Left, right, straight, far, close. Navigate any city with confidence.
